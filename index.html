About Us

At Fatty House, we believe fast food should be more than just quick—it should be delicious, satisfying, and made with care. From our juicy burgers and crispy fries to our signature shakes, every bite is crafted to bring comfort and joy. We started with one simple idea: serve bold flavors at affordable prices, in a place where everyone feels welcome. Whether you’re grabbing a meal on the go or sharing with friends, Fatty is here to make every moment tasty.
